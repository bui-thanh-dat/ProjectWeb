Coconut Bundt Cake with White Chocolate-Coconut Glaze

This coconut bundt cake is the most perfect coconut cake recipe. It was flavorful and moist with the perfect amount of coconut flavor.

I don't know what it is about bundt cakes, but everyone seems to love them. I think it's because they're not as scary as a layer cake.
They remind me of something you would hit on a Sunday night to bring to grandma's house.

I love a delicious layered cake, (like this Dark Chocolate Cake with Nutella Buttercream),
or a beautiful cupcake, (like these Key Lime Cupcakes), I've always been drawn to those bundt cake.

That's why I'm so excited to share this recipe today, powered by Sommer's new e-book from a spicy perspective - Brilliant Bundt Cakes.
I was overjoyed when Sommer released this ebook, as my bundt pan wasn't fully utilized, and I knew this ebook would get that pan on a regular rotation.

Brilliant Bundt Cakes is filled with 25 bundt cake recipes - perfect for any occasion. And it doesn't stop there - you also get 10 top recipes
(for things like Fresh Blueberry Sauce or Creamy Peanut Butter Sauce) - recipes that are sure to add something special to the dish. your bundt cake.

Here are the details:

Number of images: Many! There is a photo of every recipe, and every recipe is beautiful.

Difficulty level of recipes:

Easy. Bundt cakes are known to be pretty simple, and I think all
the recipes fall into that category. As long as you can remember to grease and flour the pan well, you shouldn't have any problems.

Availability of ingredients:

Ease. You should be able to find all the ingredients
in a regular grocery store. In fact, I think I already have plenty of ingredients for some recipes right in my pantry.

Other recipes I'd like to try:


Cinnamon Swirl Bundt Cake
Toasted S'mores Bundt Cake
Italian Cream Bundt Cake with Pistachios
Peaches 'n Cream Bundt Cake

I loved the wide range of recipes in this book, and honestly changed my mind several times when I was trying to decide what to do.
I have a thing for coconuts (especially recently) so it's no surprise that I ended up with this coconut bread.

And let me tell you - I am very pleased with my choice.

This cake is everything that I would expect for a bundt cake. A moist cake with a dense yet soft crumb, this coconut bread has the perfect
amount of coconut flavor running through it. And yeast!! I'll admit I'm crazy for a simple glaze, and will dip my fingers in the puddles of glaze dripping from the cake. (Don't judge - you know you do it too.)

This glaze is very simple, but it gives a wonderful coconut flavor to this cake. My kids are not fans of coconuts (horror!!) but they still love this cake, minus the shredded coconut on top.
But my husband and I are die-hard fans, ending up with more of this cake than I'd like to admit.
I was planning on saving a slice or two and then finding a neighbor to pawn the rest, but well, that didn't happen.

I would absolutely recommend this ebook to everyone. And the timing is perfect, as I'm sure you're still looking for dessert for your holiday
parties or even for your big holiday meal. And right now, Brilliant Bundt Cakes is only $2.99. You can't beat such a deal!!
